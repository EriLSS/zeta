New York-style cheesecake

New York-style cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert that is characterized by its dense, smooth texture and sweet, tangy flavor. It is traditionally made with a graham cracker crust and a filling made from cream cheese, eggs, and sugar, and is often served with a sweet fruit compote or whipped cream.

Chocolate Lava Cake

Chocolate lava cake is a decadent dessert that is known for its rich, gooey center that oozes out when cut open. It is made with a chocolate cake batter that is slightly undercooked, creating a molten center that is both warm and indulgent.

French macaron

A French macaron is a delicate, meringue-based cookie that is made with almond flour and filled with a variety of flavors such as fruit jams, buttercream, or ganache. It is known for its crisp outer shell and soft, chewy center, and is often served as a sweet treat or a fancy dessert option.
